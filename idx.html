Leading Indian IT Cities

Bangalore

Known as the Silicon Valley of India, Bangalore is home to major IT companies like Infosys, Wipro, and numerous startups. It contributes significantly to India's software exports.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad's HITEC City hosts global giants like Microsoft, Google, and Amazon. It's a hub for IT services, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology.

Chennai

Chennai is a major IT corridor with companies like TCS, Cognizant, and Zoho. It's known for its software development and BPO services.

Pune

Pune's Hinjewadi IT Park houses firms like IBM, Accenture, and Tech Mahindra. It's emerging as a key player in automotive and IT sectors.

Mumbai

As India's financial capital, Mumbai hosts IT firms like Capgemini, Deloitte, and Reliance Jio. It's strong in fintech and media tech.

Delhi-NCR

The National Capital Region includes Gurgaon and Noida, home to HCL, Adobe, and Flipkart. It's a hotspot for e-commerce and IT consulting.
